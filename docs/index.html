Crowd-source paper reading

29 Nov, 2017 general

In summer 2017 during one of the Xin He lab reading group session we brought up the notion of crowd-source paper reading. The gist is that we should figure out a way to not only share our literature reading notes, but also engage in discussions / debates if there are multiple people interested in the same paper. We were excited about the idea. But how do we go about doing it?
